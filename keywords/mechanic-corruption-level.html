Corruption Level (腐化等级)

Every 20 levels increase gear drops in battle by 1, up to 2.
Each level reduces total DMG taken and HP Loss by 1 for enemies.
Each level increases total DMG dealt and total HP restored by 1 for enemies.
Each level increases status stacks applied by 1 for enemies.
Each level increases Action Mana by 1 for enemies.

CN: 每20级使战斗装备掉落数量+1，最多+2。
每级使敌方角色受到全部伤害和失去生命-1。
每级使敌方角色造成全部伤害和恢复+1。
每级使敌方角色造成全部状态层数+1。
每级使敌方角色行动回魔+1。
